8.) Quadratic Equation

Finally, we introduce the a general form of a quadratic function:

$$f(x) = ax^2 + bx +c$$

So we added a term bx. You may think of f(x) as a sum of 2 functions: G(x) = ax^2 , H(x) = bx+c. F(x ) = g(x) + h(x).
