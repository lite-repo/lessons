1.) The simplest Quadratic function has the form:

We will just look at $$x^2$$

$$f(x) = x^2$$

Let's explore the properties of this function by looking at its graph .

2.) Let's introduce a slightly more general quadratic function:

We will just multiply $$x^2$$ by a constant number.

$$f(x) = 2x^2$$

What happened to the graph? Does it still look like a parabola? Is it "narrower" or "wider" than $$x^2$$ parabola? Still a parabola, but narrower. It is because for each value of $$x$$, the value of $$2x^2$$ is twice the value of the basic (canonical) parabola.
