2.) Let's introduce a bit more general quadratic function:

We will just multiply x^2 by a constant number.

$$f(x) = 2x^2$$

QQ: What happened to the graph? Does it still look like a parabola? Is it "narrower" or "wider" than x^2 parabola? Still a parabola, but narrower. It is because for each value of x, the value of 2x^2 is twice the value of the basic (canonical) parabola.
